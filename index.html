About Me

Discover my journey, passion, and what drives me to build intelligent solutions.

I'm Rahul Kumar, an aspiring Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer with a strong foundation in Business Analytics. Currently pursuing my MBA in Business Analytics at Uttaranchal University, I'm deeply passionate about harnessing the power of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science to solve complex, real-world business challenges.

My journey into the world of data began with a B.Sc. in Agriculture, where I developed a keen eye for analytical thinking, scientific reasoning, and data-driven research methodology. This unique background gives me a distinctive perspective — blending domain knowledge with cutting-edge technology to deliver holistic solutions.

I specialize in building end-to-end Machine Learning pipelines, from data collection and feature engineering to model training, evaluation, and deployment. My project portfolio includes customer segmentation systems, AI-powered recommendation engines, predictive analytics models, and interactive business intelligence dashboards using Power BI.

Beyond technical skills, I bring strong business acumen developed through my MBA studies. I understand how to translate data insights into actionable business strategies, whether it's optimizing marketing campaigns, improving customer retention, or streamlining operational efficiency. I believe the best data scientists are those who can bridge the gap between technical complexity and business impact.

I'm a continuous learner with multiple industry certifications from Microsoft, Google, Coursera, and Snowflake. I actively engage with cloud platforms like Google Cloud, Vertex AI, BigQuery, Snowflake, and Databricks to stay at the forefront of modern data infrastructure. My commitment to open-source contribution on GitHub reflects my belief in collaborative learning and knowledge sharing.

When I'm not building ML models or crafting dashboards, you'll find me exploring the latest research in generative AI, experimenting with new frameworks, or writing about data science concepts. I thrive in collaborative environments and am always eager to take on challenging problems that push the boundaries of what's possible with data.
